Jump to: 16th Nov


16th November 2022 @ 20:17

a couple of days ago, I finally finished a playthrough of Deltarune Chapter 1! i'm definitely no RPG expert, but I really enjoyed the game overall.

View in: JPG PNG

I had watched a couple full playthroughs of it back when it came out in 2018 and played a little bit of it in 2019, but never really finished it myself until a few months ago, when I decided to start a playthrough again and see if I could finish it. It's not a particularly long game by any means, but I tend to play games in small chunks, which is why it can take me longer to finish most games.

The original playthroughs I did were on the Nintendo Switch and on my main laptop (2017 MacBook Pro), but for this playthrough I decided to use my 2008 15 inch MacBook Pro running OS X 10.9 Mavericks, which I did a video on a while ago (YouTube link). It runs just fine using the original version (the one with Chapter 1 only), since the newer version with both Chapter 1 + 2 seems to require OS X 10.11 El Capitan as a minimum. (if you're looking for the original version, it has been archived here)
